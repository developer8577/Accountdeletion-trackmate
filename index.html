Account Deletion — TrackMate

Delete Your Account
At TrackMate, we respect your right to control your personal information. You can request deletion of your account at any time. Please read the following information before proceeding.
1. How to Delete Your Account
Contact our support team and include the requested details.

Contact: amanpreetb@estreet.in

Include the following information in your request:

  • Your registered mobile number
  • Your user type (User A or User B)
  • A brief confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and process the deletion.

2. What Happens After Account Deletion
Important consequences of permanent deletion.
  • Your personal information — mobile number, user type, and location data — will be permanently removed from our systems.
  • You will no longer be able to access TrackMate features or services.
  • Any previously shared location data with other users will no longer be available.
  • This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your account cannot be restored.
3. Data Retention
What we may keep and why.

In some cases, we may retain certain non-personal or anonymized data for legal or operational purposes, but this data cannot be traced back to you.

4. Need Help?
Questions or issues with deletion.

If you face any issues with deleting your account or have questions, please contact our support team at amanpreetb@estreet.in.
